About the Game

Do you wish to make virtual donuts and even have them made for you while sleeping? Well this game is for you!

Just click on the big donut to get started. When You collect enough donuts, you'll unlock the ability to purchase auto-clickers and donut multipliers with the donuts you've made.

Auto-clickers will make donuts for you each second. One auto-clicker will add one donut per second, two auto-clickers will add 2 donuts per second, etc.

Donut multipliers will increase the number of donuts made with each click (auto-clicks included). Each multiplier increases the value of a click by 20%.
