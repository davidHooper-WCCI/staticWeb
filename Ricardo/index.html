The Baking Company by Fred the Baker. After many years of making delicious donuts, Fred would like your help in making fresh new goodies for everybody to enjoy. CLICK on the Donut to make as many goodies as possible, the sky is the limit!

Donut Maker was inspired by the game, Cookie Clicker. Cookie Clicker is a fun interactive Javascript game appropriate for all ages. The goal of the game is to make cookies by clicking on the cookie. Once you earn enough cookies, you can buy upgrades to help you make cookies faster. Cookie Clicker allows the user to have a fun experience that appears to be endless.
